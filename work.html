Kafo

Kafo is an API-centeric mobile application using data from Translink and Google Maps in order to determine if you can get a coffee before your bus arrives.

Using real-time transit data and bus stops around the area, Kafo calculates which busses are available. Then, the user can choose which shop they want to go to, and Kafo calculates the walking and ordering time compared to when the bus will arrive.

  • Client: Student project
  • Date: December 2017
  • Check it out in Github: Kafo

Band Poster

This was created as a mock poster for the band the National for their Vancouver tour dates for a Photoshop class. The class had a certain amount of layer styles, blending, and textures that were required.

The woman represents a spirit of the land who has been displaced by urbanization. To create this image, three images were composited: the antlers, the woman, and a secondary coat to fix cropping issues with the coat. Additional details come from the tree photos and graphic elements developed in Illustrator.

  • Client: School Project
  • Date: December 2016

ACL Fact Sheet

This was created as a project for an Illustrator course after four of my friends suffered ACL tears playing sports.

The information on this poster was heavily researched, and shows the latest information according to the latest sports science. The design was inspired by medical anatomy posters, with a brighter twist to give a positive, helpful tone to the message delivery.

  • Client: School project
  • Date: April 2017

Sonorous

Sonorous from evelyn cranston on Vimeo.

Sonorous was created when our team was challenged with creating a video that told a story with only natural sounds — no narrative, music, or dialogue.

We made intentional choices in the repetition and pacing of shots and sounds in order for the story to make sense without the aid of narrative.

We used professional grade equipment to film, then edited and adjusted audio, color, and sequencing & transitions in Premiere Pro.

  • Client: School Project
  • Date: September 2017

Lifekeep

We filmed with professional cameras and a drone. The special effects were created in Adobe After Effects, and we edited and adjusted audio, color, and transitions in Premiere Pro.

  • Client: School project
  • Date: December 2017
